Nutrition

A balanced diet fuels your body and mind. Focus on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid too much processed food.

Exercise

Daily movement strengthens your body and reduces health risks. Even a 30-minute walk or stretching makes a difference.

Sleep

Good sleep helps recovery and focus. Aim for 7 to 9 hours per night and keep a regular schedule.
